Hi, I'm Audrey 👋

I built Pawfolio because of Flappy — my Miniature Schnauzer who inspired every feature in this app.

I wanted one place to keep every vet visit, treatment schedule and expense without juggling notes, spreadsheets or half-remembered dates.

As pet owners, we can always do better — because they give us their whole heart, and they deserve nothing less in return. 🐾💛
